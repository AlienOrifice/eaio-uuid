4    * What should the client do if a call to cassandra node fails and we suspect that the node is

12   * {@value #ON_FAIL_TRY_ALL_AVAILABLE} will try all available servers in the cluster before giving
13   * up and returning the communication error to the user.
14   *
15   */

22    /** On communication error try all known servers before giving up */
